Air Freight Services

Sameday Domestic Air Freight

BlackHaul can offer sameday Domestic door to door Air Freight between Perth, Brisbane, Adelaide, Melbourne and Sydney. Other locations are available upon request. A shipment will have to be picked up in the morning in order for delivery to be arranged for the afternoon/evening.

Overnight Domestic Air Freight

A consignment can be picked up in the afternoon, and delivered on the afternoon or morning of the following day. Subject to available flights.

Port to Port Domestic Air Services

We can arrange for a consignment to be dropped off at an Air Freight terminal around Australia, and/or picked up on the other end, saving the high cost of first and last mile delivery, and saving time. 

Economy Domestic Air Freight

Delivery within a few days. Economy Domestic Air is a lot quicker than road freight, however it is slightly more expensive.

SMALL PARCEL, SATCHEL & MULTIPLE CARTONS

E-Commerce Economy Road Freight

Courier Services

Courier services are available in every State Capital City, excluding Darwin, Canberra and Hobart. Services in these regions may be available upon request.

Sameday Courier

Pickup after 10AM, 11AM, 12AM, or 1PM, and delivery within the same day. Same city distribution and weekend services are also available

P2P Courier

A direct, point to point courier for urgent deliveries. Delivery before a cutoff point, P2P courier is available until late evening, however increased rates may apply

International Imports + Exports

International freight to and from Australia is available by specialized sea and freight channels. Also available for individual shipments with no minimum weight, as well as dropshipping fulfilment services.

Warehousing

3PL, Pick and Pack, and order fulfilment through BlackHaul's network or another third party courier company
